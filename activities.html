Activities

Farm Tours

By attending a formal tour, you will be able to hear the stories behind the designs and construction taking place. Participants will hear about many elements currently in place and others in development from hot composting to organic food, paddock to plate restaurant and fermentation, animal integration, market gardens, KidFarmer,learning experiences through Giving Tree and lots more.

Cooking with the stars

People who attend the fetival wil be treated to learn how to cook dishes with the Rosssella plant by first watching Glen Barratt and others, how they get the job done and then by applying what you've leanred!

Face Paintings

Kids which come to the festival will be treated to facepaintings of animals and plants which are grown on the farm. They will also have a chnace to learn more about the Rossella plant while they get their face painted!

Farmer Talks Q&A's

Both kids and adults will have a chnace at asking question to all the farmers that came to finding out how to run a farm, how to grow the Rosella plant and what makes the plant unique.
